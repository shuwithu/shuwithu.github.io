An XR-based wellness platform that empowers healthy aging through interactive, data-driven games and personalized biofeedback. It offers three core modules:

  • Serious Game for Cognitive Ability Monitoring: A gamified cognitive assessment, leveraging room-escape challenges and spatial memory tasks to identify early signs of cognitive decline.
  • Symptom Management for Motor Control Decline: Adaptive exercise programs, guided by biosensors, to support physical health and prevent falls, tailored to individual biometrics.
  • Automated Mental Health Consultation: Virtual mental health sessions led by AI-driven avatars, offering guided meditation and emotional support. This integrative approach promotes holistic well-being, enhancing mental sharpness and resilience in older adults.
